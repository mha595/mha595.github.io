The Trip

My cousin, Hamzah, from Germany came with us on the Sydney trip. It was a looooong drive. But not TOO long. First we went to MELBOURNE... then we went to Sydney. To make it clear, Hamzah went to Melbourne, then after a few days, WE went there, and, last but not least, we all went to Sydney. Ya get it? Never mind, let us continues So one of the things we did there was ride a awesome ride on a ferry. We rode it for little while and then we rode under Harbour Bridge. Just 'round the corner we found... the Sydney Opera House! When we reached the dock, we got off and ate McDonalds. Oh, and when I was holding a chip, I was about to eat it, but a bird swooped down behind me and SNATCHED the chip right out of my hand! Oh, that bird will regret it... Aaaanyway, on with the story. So, we walked to the Sydney Opera House, and we also got ice-cream on the way.
